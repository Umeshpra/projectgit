Manali – Stream of Beas River


The Beas River is the most attractive and beautiful sights in the Manali valley. The beautiful view and rippling sound of the river fascinate the lots of tourists. You may feel that you are in heaven when you will seat on a stone and water flowing over your feet. So don’t miss the Beas River whenever you visit Manali.

Goa Arambol Beach


Arambol Beach is one of most charming beaches in Goa which is located near about 50 Kms from Panaji. Popularity of Arambol Beach is growing day by day due to its scenic beauty and wide beach of sand.
